Request Data Deletion

If you have used the MindCare application and would like your account and all associated data to be permanently deleted, you may request it at any time.

How to Request Deletion

To request data deletion, please email us at dasettisribalu@gmail.com from the email address registered with your MindCare account. Please include your name and state that you would like to delete your account data.

We will process your request and permanently delete all your information within 7 business days.
